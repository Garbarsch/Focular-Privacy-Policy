Privacy Policy for Focular

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Focular is a productivity-focused browser extension designed to help users manage time spent on selected websites. We are committed to protecting your privacy and minimizing data collection.

Consent

By selecting "I Consent" within the extension, you agree to participate and allow Focular to log limited usage data as described below.

Information We Collect

Focular logs only the following information:

  • Time spent on websites that you explicitly add as trigger sites.

The extension does not log browsing activity for websites that you have not added as trigger sites.

Information We Do NOT Collect

Focular does not collect:

  • Browsing history beyond selected trigger sites
  • Page content
  • Text you type
  • Passwords
  • Form inputs
  • Personal identifiers

User Control

You can add, edit, or remove trigger sites at any time through the Focular extension popup.

Data Storage

All logged data is processed and stored locally within your browser unless explicitly stated otherwise. Focular does not sell, transfer, or share your data with third parties.
